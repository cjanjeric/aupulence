ABOUT AUPULENCE

Ownership Background


Mr. Cruz is the owner of an education counselling company based in Canberra that specializes in giving personalized guidance to students navigating the college/university admissions process. He has over seven years of experience working as an education counsellor in partnership with top universities and colleges all over Australia.

He holds a Qualified Education Agent Counsellor (QEAC) certificate with a license number of M241. In addition to his formal education and certificate, Mark regularly attends online workshops to stay current in college/university admissions trends and best practices. He also has developed strong communication and interpersonal skills, which allows him to build strong relationships with his clients and help them feel confident and supported throughout the college/university application process

MISSION

Our mission is to provide students with personalized and comprehensive education counseling services that empower them to achieve their academic and career goals.

Our goal is to provide exceptional services and support that exceeds our clients’ expectations and helps them achieve the full potential.

VISION

Our vision is to be the leading provider of personalized and comprehensive education counseling services that inspire and empower students to achieve their full potential.
